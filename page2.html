Traditional Branches

The Traditional Branches Of Engineering Are:




1.Computer Engineering:Technical knowledge, hardware design, software design,Information technology, technology industry, engineering industry


2. Mechanical Engineering Career Paths: Automotive engineer, aerospace engineer, HVAC engineer, robotics engineer, manufacturing engineer, product design engineer.Industries: Automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, energy, robotics.


3. Electrical and Electronics Engineering Career Paths: Electrical design engineer, electronics engineer, control systems engineer, power systems engineer, telecommunication engineer, embedded systems engineer.Industries: Energy, telecommunications, consumer electronics, power generation and distribution, automation.



4. Civil Engineering Career Paths: Structural engineer, construction manager, transportation engineer, geotechnical engineer, urban planner, environmental engineer.Industries: Construction, urban development, infrastructure, environmental services.



5. Chemical Engineering Career Paths: Process engineer, petrochemical engineer, pharmaceutical engineer, materials scientist, environmental engineer.Industries: Chemicals, pharmaceuticals, energy, food and beverage, biotechnology.
